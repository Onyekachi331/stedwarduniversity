LIST OF COURSE

Civil Engineering

This course provides students with a strong foundation in mathematics, physics, and engineering principles, alongside practical skills in project management, environmental sustainability, and structural analysis.

Medical Doctor

The Medical Doctor (MD) course is a comprehensive program designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and clinical experience required to practice medicine. The curriculum covers foundational sciences, clinical medicine, patient care, medical ethics, and research.

Computer Science

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental concepts of computer science. Students will learn about algorithms, programming languages, software development, data structures, computer hardware, networking, databases, and cybersecurity.

Accountant

This accounting course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles and practices of financial and managerial accounting. Students will learn how to record, classify, and interpret financial transactions, prepare financial statements, and analyze business performance.

Public Admin.

This course provides an introduction to the principles and practices of public administration. Students will explore the structure, functions, and processes of government agencies, focusing on policy development, organizational management, leadership, and ethical decision-making.

Law/Admin Course

Mass Communication Course

Medical Science Course

The course covers subjects like anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, pathology, pharmacology, and microbiology. Students learn how the human body functions, how diseases develop, and how they are diagnosed and treated.

Political Science Course

The course covers topics such as political theory, comparative politics, international relations, public administration, and policy analysis. Students learn how governments function, how laws are made and enforced, and how political decisions impact society.

About Us

St. Edward University is a leading institution committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development. Our university offers a diverse range of programs, world-class faculty, and a vibrant campus environment that fosters learning, leadership, and personal growth. At St. Edward University, we empower students to achieve their goals and make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.


List of Courses We Offer:

  • Civil Engineering: Focuses on designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure such as roads, bridges, and buildings. Students learn structural analysis, project management, and environmental sustainability.
  • Medical Doctor (MD): Prepares students for medical practice through studies in anatomy, physiology, clinical medicine, patient care, and medical ethics.
  • Computer Science: Covers programming, algorithms, software development, data structures, networking, databases, and cybersecurity.
  • Mass Communication: Covers journalism, public relations, advertising, media ethics, and communication theories for careers in media and broadcasting.
  • Political Science: Studies anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, pathology, pharmacology, and microbiology, focusing on human health and disease.
  • Law: Includes constitutional law, criminal law, contract law, property law, and legal ethics, focusing on legal research and argumentation.
